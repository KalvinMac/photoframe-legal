Frequently asked questions

Is my data private?

Yes. Your events, tasks, habits, and countdowns are stored only on your device, and the AI features run on-device. See our Privacy Policy for the full detail.

Do I need an account?

No. You can use Pocket Mondays entirely as a guest. Sign in with Apple is optional and only personalizes the app; your credentials stay on your device.

How do I scan a schedule from a photo?

Open the add-event flow and choose the scan option, then take a photo of the schedule or pick one from your library. The app reads it on-device and creates the events for you to review before saving.

Why isn't my event showing on the Lock Screen (Live Activity)?

Make sure Live Activities are enabled in Settings → Pocket Mondays and in Settings → Face ID & Passcode → Live Activities. Live Activities appear around the time an event starts and clear when it ends.

My Apple Calendar events aren't syncing.

Check that Calendar sync is turned on in the app's settings and that you granted Calendar access in Settings → Pocket Mondays. If you recently changed the permission, reopen the app to let it refresh.

Weather or holidays aren't loading.

These features need an internet connection. Weather also needs Location permission. Everything else in the app works fully offline.

How do I delete my data?

Delete individual items inside the app, or remove the app from your device to erase all of its local data.

Requirements

  • iPhone running the latest iOS.
  • On-device AI features require a device that supports Apple Intelligence.
  • Apple Watch app requires a paired Apple Watch.
