Everything a photo frame should be — and more

Built around the things you actually want on a screen sitting in your home.

🖼️

Beautiful Slideshows

Full-screen photos and videos from any album or your whole library, with shuffle, adjustable timing, and a graceful Ken Burns motion effect.

🕒

Clock, Date & Place

An elegant overlay shows the time, the date a photo was taken, and the place it was captured — so every memory has context.

📹

Live HomeKit Cameras

View one or all of your HomeKit security cameras live, right from the frame. Tap “Back to Frame” to return to your photos.

💡

Smart-Home Controls

Control lights, locks, switches, garage doors, and fans by room. Your whole home, organized on one calm dashboard.

🎯

Motion Tracking

A doorbell or motion sensor can switch the frame to the right camera, then return to your photos automatically after a delay you set.

🔒

Private by Design

No accounts, no servers, no analytics. Your photos and HomeKit data never leave your device. Privacy isn't a setting — it's the default.

See it in action

A glance at the frame, the dashboard, and the controls.

A frame that fades into the background

Tap once for playback controls and a quick-access bar. The rest of the time, it's just your photos — clock and caption included.

Every camera, one tap away

Bring up a live grid of your HomeKit cameras — front door, garage, patio, and more — without ever leaving the room.

Watch and act in the moment

Open a single camera and the controls for that room appear right below it — unlock a door or flip on the porch light while you watch.

Your home, room by room

A clean controls screen groups every accessory by room, so the right switch is always exactly where you'd expect it.

Set it up in seconds

Choose your source and albums, set the interval, toggle the clock and motion effects, and pick which HomeKit devices appear. Then let it run.

What stays on your device

Photos & videosRead for display only. Never uploaded or copied off your device.
HomeKit dataHandled by Apple's HomeKit framework, directly between your device and accessories.
No trackingNo analytics, no advertising, no third-party SDKs — at all.
No accountsNothing to sign in to, and no servers of our own.
